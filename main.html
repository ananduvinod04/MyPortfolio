About

Hello! I'm Anandu, a Biotechnology graduate who unexpectedly found a deep love for web development. My journey might seem unconventional—science to accounting to coding—but each step has fueled my curiosity and problem-solving mindset. What started as a weekend hobby soon turned into late-night coding sessions, and now I’m diving deep into the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) while building solid foundations in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I’ve already built a few projects like a personal portfolio and a simple e-commerce layout, and I’m currently working on a full-stack blog app. I'm also brushing up on tools like Git, REST APIs, and eager to explore TypeScript. With four years of experience in accounting, I bring attention to detail and a love for structured problem-solving. My goal? To transition into a full-time developer role, collaborate on exciting projects, and maybe even contribute to open-source communities. Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring nature, binge-watching tech talks, and experimenting with new recipes—because creativity shouldn't just stay in the IDE, right? Let's connect and build something amazing together!
